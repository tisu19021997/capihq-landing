Stage 1 of 5

Capture

Work happens in Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, or added directly.

What gets captured
✓Prompts, documents, and chat threads
✓Decisions, outputs, and feedback
✓Screenshots, files, and references
✓Manual notes and team inputs
i Why it matters

The more context we capture, the smarter everything downstream becomes.

Stage 2 of 5

Learn

Recurring patterns across people and teams are recognized.

What gets recognized
✓Prompts and workflows that repeat
✓The same steps done by different people
✓Tools and files that keep showing up together
✓Gaps: things people need that don't exist yet
i Why it matters

A pattern only counts once it shows up across people, not once.

Stage 3 of 5

Review

A human checks it, or it's auto-approved when it passes the guardrails an admin set.

What gets checked
✓The real sessions behind the pattern
✓Who it came from, and how often
✓What it would replace or update
✓Ownership, risk, and access
i Why it matters

Nothing publishes without a reason attached to it.

Stage 4 of 5

Publish

The approved capability becomes a new, versioned release.

What gets released
✓A named, versioned capability
✓Full history back to version one
✓A record of who approved it and when
✓A one-click path back to the prior version
i Why it matters

Nothing is overwritten, so nothing is ever truly lost.

Stage 5 of 5

Reuse

Any AI app on your team can discover and use it.

What becomes available
✓Search from inside any connected AI app
✓The same capability, every time it's needed
✓Adoption and usage, tracked automatically
✓A head start for anyone new to the team
i Why it matters

The next person starts from what already works, not from zero.
